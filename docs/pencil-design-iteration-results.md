## Recommendation

Use `synthesis-compelling-mock` as the next production-oriented base.

Borrow from the other directions deliberately:

- From Ive: the calm hero/status object and anxiety-reducing copy.
- From Victor: the interactive planner idea — trip objects + rolling 180-day lens + live risk preview.
- From Tufte: compact proof ledger and days-returning explanation for the report/explanation layer.
- From Rams: decisive labels and seriousness for current-status/pro mode.
- From Trust Instrument: accessibility floor, bullet KPI, and public-service credibility.
